Tempest

by DISC1PLE

In their many years of traveling the Misty Sea, some Orchids discovered an island southeast of Gloomhaven that soon became renowned for its calamitous thunderstorms. In times of extreme conflict, volunteers are selected to break the norm of peaceful meditation in order to pursue an arcane source of raw power. They travel to the stormy island and climb its towering, rocky peaks, allowing their bodies to be struck by lightning repeatedly while they study its electrical energy. Through meditation and self-discipline in the midst of personal suffering, these Orchids achieve a perfect union with the storm, enabling them to wield lightning itself in weaponized form, and even to repurpose the electrical energy of the living and dead to augment their abilities.

This lightning-fast, storm-themed class introduces a new status effect named Dodge, enabling the Tempest to evade incoming attacks. It's extreme speed often allows it act before its enemies, but this strength can also frequently make it the target of enemy attacks. You will need to plan your turn carefully and position yourself strategically to deal damage to your enemies without taking a beating as a low-health melee character. The Tempest also utilizes a personal resource called Sparks, which drop on each enemy's death and can be spent to augment and perform abilities.
