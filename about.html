Goals of the Community

To unify mechanics and concepts, such as custom statuses, to be balanced and carried over into the wider designs of varied custom content.

To help avoid overlap on ideas and keep custom content fresh and varied, allowing those with similar ideas to help collaborate and/or differentiate.

To give all custom content creators a place to easily get feedback and help on testing and reviewing their ideas.
