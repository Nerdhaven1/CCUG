Woebound

by Quasilocal

Many of Woebound's top abilities follow a structured pattern in regard to the elements that appear on it, which is designed to drive a healthy flow of elements and maintain options on each turn. These include negative penalties from mandatory element consumption, primary and secondary element consumption bonuses, and element generation.

Not all abilities follow this rigid pattern, but it will quickly become a familiar pattern when playing the class, and this helps quickly parse each ability despite the amount of different element interactions on each card, which may seem a lot at first glance.

Typical gameplay involves using your bottom actions to consume elements that would otherwise give you a penalty on your top action. Woebound is a bit of a jack-of-all-trades, with many different ways to build and play the class depending on the role you want her to fill in your party.
